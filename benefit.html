Natural Air Purifiers

Integrating plants into your home does more than just decorate; it improves air quality by removing toxins and boosting your mental well-being.

Snake Plant

Health Benefit: Known for converting CO2 into oxygen at night, making it ideal for bedrooms to improve sleep quality.

Purification: Filters toxins like formaldehyde and benzene from the air.

Aloe Vera

Health Benefit: The gel inside leaves can heal burns and skin irritations.

Purification: Helps clear the air of pollutants found in chemical cleaners and paints.

Peace Lily

Health Benefit: Increases room humidity, which can help alleviate dry skin and respiratory issues.

Purification: One of the top plants for removing common indoor VOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds).
